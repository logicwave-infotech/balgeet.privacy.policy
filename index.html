1 Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information from Children

🔒 Balgeet does NOT collect any personal information from children. We do not ask for or store names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, photographs, audio recordings, or any other information that could personally identify a child or family member.

1.2 Non-Personal & Technical Information (Automatically Collected)

To keep the app running smoothly and to display appropriate advertisements, the app and its third-party partners may automatically collect limited, non-personal technical data, including:

  • Device type and model (e.g., Android phone or tablet)
  • Operating system version
  • App version currently in use
  • General, anonymised usage patterns (e.g., which songs are streamed most often)
  • Crash reports and error logs (used only to fix technical bugs)
  • Advertising Identifier (Google Advertising ID / GAID) — used only to serve child-safe, family-appropriate ads. See Section 5.

None of this technical data personally identifies your child. It is collected solely to maintain app performance and to serve compliant, family-safe advertisements.

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device. Links to privacy policies of third-party service providers:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Google)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

2 How We Use Your Information

The limited technical data described above is used only for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the app works correctly on your device
  • To identify and fix technical errors and crashes
  • To improve app performance and the overall user experience
  • To deliver family-safe, child-appropriate advertisements in compliance with Google Play Families Policy (see Section 5)
  • To understand general, anonymised usage trends and plan new songs and features
⚠️ We do NOT use any data to: build profiles of individual children, send marketing emails, share or sell data to third parties for their own commercial purposes, or serve targeted behavioural advertising to children.

3 Children's Privacy — Special Protections

Balgeet is directed at children under the age of 8. We take our responsibilities under child data protection laws extremely seriously.

3.1 COPPA Compliance (USA)

For users in the United States, we comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age
  • We do not require children to provide personal information to use the app
  • If we discover that a child under 13 has inadvertently provided personal information, we will immediately delete it from our records
  • Parents or guardians may contact us at any time to review, delete, or stop further data collection (see Section 8)

3.2 GDPR-K Compliance (EU / UK)

For users in the European Union and United Kingdom, we comply with GDPR requirements applicable to children's data. We treat all Balgeet users as children for data protection purposes, applying the highest available safeguards. We process data only on the legal bases of legitimate interest (app functionality) and, where required, parental consent.

3.3 Google Play Families Policy

Balgeet participates in the Google Play Families programme and is bound by Google's Families Policy:

  • Only advertisements compliant with Google's Families Ads requirements are shown (see Section 5)
  • We do not use persistent device identifiers to track or profile children
  • We do not link data collected in Balgeet to data from other apps without explicit parental consent
  • All app content has been reviewed and confirmed appropriate for children aged 0 to 8 years

3.4 No Account Creation Required

Balgeet does not require children or parents to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to stream songs. The app is fully functional without registration.

4 Family Policy & Children's Safety

Our Commitment: Every interaction your child has with Balgeet must be safe, positive, joyful, and age-appropriate. This is non-negotiable for us.

4.1 Child-Safe Content

All content in Balgeet — Gujarati nursery rhymes, baby songs, bal geet, lyrics, animations, and visuals — has been carefully created for children aged 0 to 8 years:

  • All songs celebrate Gujarati culture and heritage in a safe, age-appropriate way
  • Animations and visuals are colourful, gentle, and designed for young children
  • No violent, sexual, disturbing, or inappropriate content exists anywhere in the app
  • No user-generated content is permitted — eliminating exposure to harmful material from other users
  • 🌙 Dark Mode is provided for gentle nighttime use, protecting little eyes at bedtime

4.2 Educational Purpose

Balgeet is designed with early childhood development in mind. Singing nursery rhymes from as early as 6 months helps children develop language skills, pronunciation, and memory. Our unique real-time sing-along lyrics feature helps children learn Gujarati naturally and joyfully — connecting them to their mother tongue whether they are in Gujarat, Mumbai, London, Toronto, or Dubai.

4.3 No In-App Purchases or External Links

Balgeet contains no in-app purchases, paid upgrades, virtual currencies, or links to external websites and social media platforms that could expose children to uncontrolled content.

Balgeet is free to use and supported by light, family-friendly advertisements. Our app uses Google's AdMob advertising platform to display advertisements.

5.1 Family-Safe Ads Only

All advertisements in Balgeet strictly comply with Google Play's Families Ads Policy:

  • Only family-safe, child-appropriate advertisements are served
  • No advertisements contain sexual, violent, or inappropriate content of any kind
  • No advertisements promote alcohol, tobacco, gambling, or adult products
  • No deceptive or manipulative techniques are used to target children
  • Advertisements are clearly distinguishable from app content

You can review Google's AdMob Privacy Policy here.

5.2 Advertising Identifiers

Advertising is delivered through Google-certified, Families-compliant ad networks. Behavioural or interest-based profiling of children is not permitted. Ad networks are contractually required to serve only family-safe content within our app.
